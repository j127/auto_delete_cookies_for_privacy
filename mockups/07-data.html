Design notes — 07 · Data

Direction: internal-tool density for people who read tables for a living: toolbar nav, filter chips, zebra striping, per-column keep-flags right in the expression grid, popup as a stat strip plus two mini-tables. This is the advanced-first counterpoint to the other nine directions — nothing folded away. Typography: small system sans with monospace for every domain and number; the Mono option converts the whole UI. Palettes: Steel blue (default), Graphite mono, Moss green. Site data: all six types permanently visible with a master header checkbox; cookies pinned on and explained.
