Design notes — 06 · Dock

Direction: a productivity-app shell — icon rail with hover tooltips for settings navigation, and a popup composed purely of uniform rows (icon, label + caption, trailing control). Rows make the popup feel like a native panel rather than a web page. Typography: compact system sans; the alternate stack leans Segoe/Roboto for a Windows-native feel. Palettes: Harbor blue-grey (default), Plum for a friendlier violet, Sand as a warm neutral nodding to the logo. Site data: master toggle row with an Advanced accordion on a recessed band.
