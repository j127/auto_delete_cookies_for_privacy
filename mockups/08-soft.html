Design notes — 08 · Soft

Direction: the warm consumer-SaaS take — floating cards with soft shadows on a tinted canvas, oversized toggles, pill navigation, copy that talks like a person. Typography: rounded system stack for friendliness; plain sans as the safer alternative. Palettes: Sky blue (default), Peach (warm, logo-adjacent), Lavender. Site data: the Simple ↔ Advanced pill switch — Simple keeps five friendly rows, Advanced expands timing plus a full per-type card, all inline.
