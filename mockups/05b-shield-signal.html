Design notes — 05b · Shield / Signal

Direction: a palette study, not a redesign — the 05-shield trust dashboard verbatim, defaulting to the conservative blue "Signal" palette borrowed from 02-console (its full light and dark sets, including status colors). Blue reads institutional and calm where Guardian's teal reads consumer-privacy; Midnight stays as the darkest-suit option. Typography: unchanged from 05. Site data: unchanged — preset cards with a Custom fold.
