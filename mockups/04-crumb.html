Design notes — 04 · Crumb

Direction: the brand-forward option — the logo's tan cookie and red prohibition ring become the product's whole personality: warm paper surfaces, pill shapes, soft dashes instead of hard rules, a touch of playful copy ("crumbs"). Typography: rounded system stack by default; the serif option gives headings a bakery-sign warmth. Palettes: Bakery is the logo verbatim; Cocoa deepens it into brown-on-cream; Berry tests a jammier red. Site data: master "Delete all site data" toggle on a highlighted cookie-tinted band, with an Advanced accordion of individually framed types.
