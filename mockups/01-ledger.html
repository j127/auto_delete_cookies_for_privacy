Design notes — 01 · Ledger

Direction: the calm, airy end of professional SaaS: fixed sidebar, stacked setting cards, one accent used sparingly. Typography: humanist system sans for everything; the optional serif heading stack adds an editorial voice without loading fonts. Palettes: Indigo is the neutral SaaS default; Bakery carries the product logo's tan/red identity; Evergreen tests a trust-signaling green. Site data: master "Delete all site data" toggle with an Advanced accordion listing each type in plain language.
