Design notes — 02 · Console

Direction: the fast, keyboard-adjacent utility look — flat surfaces, hairline dividers, 32px rows, tabular numerals, keycap hints. Dark is the default because this direction is at home there. Typography: compact system sans; the Mono option leans into the terminal personality for domains and counts. Palettes: Graphite-violet as the signature look, Signal blue as the conservative option, Mint for a fresher take. Site data: page-level Simple ↔ Advanced switch — Simple shows five plain-language rows, Advanced expands timing and the per-type list inline.
