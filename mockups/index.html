Auto-Delete Cookies for Privacy — design mockups

Ten design directions for the new popup and settings UI. Open a mockup and use its design panel to explore modes, palettes, fonts, radius, and density.

01 · Ledger

Airy SaaS: sidebar nav, stacked cards, generous whitespace. Site data via master toggle + Advanced accordion.

02 · Console

Dark-first compact utility: dense rows, hairlines, keycap hints. Page-level Simple ↔ Advanced switch.

03 · Mono

Sharp monochrome minimalism, uppercase micro-labels. "What's included?" explainer + Customize fold.

04 · Crumb

Warm brand direction built from the logo's tan cookie + red ring; pill shapes, friendly copy.

05 · Shield

Trust dashboard: protection hero, weekly stats, plain-language cleanup-level preset cards.

05b · Shield / Signal

05-shield exactly as designed, defaulting to the blue Signal palette from 02-console.

05c · Shield / Cookie mark

The cookie-prohibition logo replaces the shield in the hero, brand, and primary action.

05d · Shield / Simple popup

Final copy pass: "Auto-delete is on / This site won't remember you.", Keep-family naming (no whitelist/greylist jargon), scoped clean labels, Share menu in the name bar; a settings checkbox reveals advanced controls plus Cookies/Storage inspector tabs (live in the mockup).

06 · Dock

Icon-rail app shell with tooltips; popup as uniform stacked rows with trailing controls.

07 · Data

Dense admin console: toolbar, filter chips, zebra tables. Advanced-first — every data type visible.

08 · Soft

Friendly modern SaaS: big rounding, floating cards, oversized toggles. Simple ↔ Advanced pill switch.

09 · Fleet

Enterprise console: dark global bar, tab strip, breadcrumbs, status dots. Cleanup-scope preset cards.

10 · Aurora

Dark-first cool neutrals with one restrained accent gradient for primary actions; glassy popup header.
