Design notes — 03 · Mono

Direction: engineering-grade minimalism — near-monochrome, hairline rules instead of shadows, sharp corners, uppercase micro-labels doing the wayfinding. Typography: system sans with monospace reserved for domains, versions, and counts; the all-Mono option pushes it further. Palettes: Ink is pure monochrome (the accent is simply the strongest ink), Carbon adds one blue, Redline borrows the logo's prohibition red as the single accent. Site data: one plain checkbox, a "What's included?" glossary disclosure, and a separate Customize fold for per-type control.
