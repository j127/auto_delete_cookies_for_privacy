Design notes — 10 · Aurora

Direction: the most contemporary of the set — cool neutral surfaces with a single accent-to-green gradient reserved for the primary action, active toggles, and the brand orb; a tinted glassy header gives the popup presence without noise. Dark-first. Typography: system sans; Rounded as the softer alternative. Palettes: Aurora indigo (default), Cyan for a cooler tone, Sunset for a warm coral take that nods to the logo's red. All gradients derive from --c-accent via color-mix, so they follow every palette. Site data: master toggle on a gradient-washed band plus an Advanced accordion.
