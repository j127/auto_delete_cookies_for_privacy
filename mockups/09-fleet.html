Design notes — 09 · Fleet

Direction: enterprise operations console — dark global bar with an environment badge, tab strip, breadcrumbs, and the classic description-left/controls-right settings grid that scales to hundreds of options. Status dots give an at-a-glance audit feel. Typography: workmanlike system sans; Mono option for ops people. Palettes: Corporate blue (default), Slate for a vendor-neutral grey, Burgundy for a distinctive private-sector look. Site data: preset radio cards (Cookies only / + storage / Everything) with an advanced fine-tune fold, framed as "Cleanup scope".
